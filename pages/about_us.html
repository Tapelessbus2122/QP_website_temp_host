About Us

The Vision

Queen’s Pharma was born from a simple observation: veterinary distribution requires more than just logistics—it requires clinical understanding. Our founders, a registered veterinarian and a licensed pharmacist, combined their expertise to ensure that every product we handle is managed with medical rigor and pharmaceutical excellence.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Transparent operations and honest partnerships.
  • Care: At the heart of every delivery is a patient waiting for relief.
  • Quality: Only the most advanced, proven therapeutics enter our catalog.
  • Compliance: Strict adherence to Hong Kong’s regulatory standards and cold-chain protocols.

    • Our Professional Services

  • Moving Beyond Distribution: We provide a full-spectrum ecosystem to support both global manufacturers and local clinics.
  • Precision Sales Targeting: Data-driven strategies to reach the right clinics for the right products.
  • B2B E-Commerce: A seamless, professional platform for vets to manage inventory and orders 24/7.
  • Cold-Chain Excellence: Our temperature-controlled delivery system ensures biologicals (like Cytopoint) maintain 100% efficacy from our warehouse to your fridge.
  • Regulatory & Registration: Expert consultation for product registration and compliance within the Hong Kong pharmaceutical landscape.
